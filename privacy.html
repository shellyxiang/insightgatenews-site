Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-09

This Privacy Policy explains how Insight Gate ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services at insightgatenews.com.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: email address, password (stored in hashed form), and basic profile information you provide when you register for an account.
  • Usage data: pages visited, actions taken on the website, time and date of visits, and basic technical information (browser type, device type, IP address).
  • Payment-related information: we do not store full payment card details ourselves. Payments and subscriptions are processed by our payment partner Creem. We may receive limited billing information from Creem such as transaction IDs, product purchased, and subscription status.
  • Communication data: content of messages you send to us via email or contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To provide access to member-only content and research report downloads.
  • To process and verify your subscription status through our payment partner.
  • To maintain the security and stability of our website.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To analyze aggregated usage patterns to improve our content and services.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how our website is used. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but some features of the site may not function properly.

4. Legal Basis (if applicable)

Depending on your location, our legal basis for processing your personal data may include: performance of a contract (providing our services to you), legitimate interests (improving our services), and your consent where required (for example, for certain cookies or marketing communications).

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers such as Creem (for payment processing) and hosting providers, solely for the purpose of operating our services.
  • Legal authorities if required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.
  • Professional advisers (e.g., accountants, lawyers) where necessary for our business operations and legal compliance.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer required, we will delete or anonymize it.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Transfers

Our services and service providers may be located in different countries. By using our website, you understand that your personal data may be transferred and processed outside of your country of residence, where data protection laws may be different.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access and obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data under certain circumstances.
  • The right to object to or restrict certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@insightgatenews.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding to your request.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@insightgatenews.com
