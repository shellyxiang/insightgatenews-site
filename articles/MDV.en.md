# Maldives — Quick Facts


- **Regional logic:** Maldives is both a South Asian island economy and a small Muslim economy.
- **Basic economic profile:** A **small island service economy** built mainly on tourism.
- **Main economic base:** Tourism is the dominant pillar, while related services, construction, and imports are also important.
- **Key structural feature:** Maldives is one of the clearest examples of a country whose economy depends heavily on **high-end tourism and external inflows**, not on manufacturing or natural resources.
- **Main current issues:** Growth remains supported by tourism, but macroeconomic imbalances, fiscal pressure, and debt risks have widened.
- **Why it matters in the map:** It helps show that the Muslim world also includes **small island tourism economies**, not only oil states or large population countries.
- **Best short reading:** A tourism-dependent island Muslim economy with strong external earnings but rising fiscal and debt pressure.
