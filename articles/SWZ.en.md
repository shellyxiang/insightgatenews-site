# Eswatini — Quick Facts

- **Regional placement:** Southern Africa, within **SADC**.
- **Geographic reality:** A **small landlocked economy** closely connected to South Africa.
- **Basic economic profile:** A small middle-income economy with manufacturing, services, and agriculture, but also high structural unemployment.
- **Main economic base:** Manufacturing, trade, agriculture, and strong dependence on **SACU revenues** and South African economic conditions.
- **Key structural feature:** Eswatini is small, but more diversified than many people assume; it is not only agricultural.
- **Main current issues:** Growth is vulnerable to swings in SACU revenues, domestic job creation is weak, and structural constraints remain significant.
- **Why it matters regionally:** Like Lesotho, it helps explain how the smaller Southern African economies are tied into the wider South African customs and trade system.
- **Best short reading:** A small Southern African economy with manufacturing and services, but one still highly dependent on regional revenue and the South African economic orbit.
