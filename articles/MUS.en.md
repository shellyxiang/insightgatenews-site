# Mauritius — Quick Facts

- **Project placement:** Southern Africa extension / Indian Ocean island economy.
- **Regional logic:** Mauritius is a **SADC member**, but it is economically quite different from the mainland Southern African resource economies.
- **Basic economic profile:** A **small upper-middle/high-income island economy** built on services, tourism, finance, and trade.
- **Main economic base:** Services dominate, especially tourism and financial services, while manufacturing and trade still matter.
- **Key structural feature:** Mauritius is one of the clearest African cases of a country that moved well beyond primary commodities into a **service-led and institutionally stronger model**.
- **Main current issues:** Growth remains resilient, but fiscal space, climate risk, and external demand weakness are now more important constraints.
- **Why it matters in the map:** It helps show that Southern Africa is not only about mining and inland economies; it also includes **Indian Ocean service economies**.
- **Best short reading:** A small Indian Ocean service economy with tourism and finance at the center, supported by relatively strong institutions.
