# Comoros — Quick Facts

- **Project placement:** Southern Africa extension / Indian Ocean island economy.
- **Regional logic:** Comoros is included in wider Southern Africa / Indian Ocean regional treatments, but it is much smaller and more fragile than Mauritius.
- **Basic economic profile:** A **small fragile island economy** with high import dependence and limited export diversification.
- **Main economic base:** Household consumption, public investment, remittances, small-scale agriculture, and a still-limited tourism and blue-economy base.
- **Key structural feature:** Comoros is one of the clearest cases of an island economy with **high vulnerability, narrow scale, and limited diversification**, rather than strong productive depth.
- **Main current issues:** Poverty remains high, import dependence is heavy, and macroeconomic resilience is still fragile.
- **Why it matters in the map:** It helps show that the Indian Ocean side of Africa includes not only successful service hubs, but also **small fragile island economies**.
- **Best short reading:** A small Indian Ocean island economy with remittances, consumption, and public investment sustaining growth, but still constrained by fragility and narrow scale.
