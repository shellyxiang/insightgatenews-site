# Somaliland — Quick Facts

- **Project placement:** Special political-economic case, best treated separately from a standard sovereign-country entry.
- **Political status:** Somaliland functions with its own institutions in practice, but it is not broadly recognized internationally as a sovereign state.
- **Regional logic:** Best grouped with the **Horn of Africa**, not with Southern or Central Africa.
- **Basic economic profile:** A **trade-, livestock-, remittance-, and port-linked economy** centered especially on Berbera.
- **Main economic base:** Livestock exports, remittances, trade, and Berbera’s port role are the clearest pillars.
- **Key structural feature:** Somaliland’s economic relevance comes less from domestic scale than from **commercial resilience, livestock trade, and port geography**.
- **Why it matters in the map:** It is useful as a **special Horn of Africa economic zone**, especially because of Berbera and livestock-export connections.
- **Best short reading:** A de facto Horn of Africa economic space built on livestock, remittances, trade, and the Berbera port corridor.
