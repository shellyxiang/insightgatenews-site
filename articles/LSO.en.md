# Lesotho — Quick Facts

- **Regional placement:** Southern Africa, clearly within the **SADC** framework.
- **Geographic reality:** A **small mountainous enclave entirely surrounded by South Africa**.
- **Basic economic profile:** A **small, highly dependent economy** closely tied to South Africa.
- **Main economic base:** Government spending, services, textiles and apparel exports, remittances, and water exports to South Africa.
- **Key structural feature:** Lesotho is one of the clearest examples of a country whose economy is deeply shaped by **dependence on a much larger neighbor**.
- **Main current issues:** Low and unstable growth, fiscal pressure, unemployment, and the need for more private-sector-led growth.
- **Why it matters regionally:** It is not important because of scale, but because it shows how **small Southern African economies are embedded in the South African economic system**.
- **Best short reading:** A small inland economy tied to South Africa through labor, trade, water, and fiscal dependence.
