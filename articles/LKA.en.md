# Sri Lanka — Quick Facts


- **Basic economic profile:** A **small but strategically located island economy** built around services, trade, tourism, and some manufacturing and agriculture.
- **Main economic base:** Tourism, transport and port activity, services, garments, and tea remain important.
- **Key structural feature:** Sri Lanka’s economy combines **port position, services, and tourism**, but remains shaped by the aftereffects of a severe balance-of-payments and debt crisis.
- **Main current issues:** The economy has been recovering, but welfare is still weak, poverty remains elevated, and reform fatigue and external shocks remain risks.

- **Best short reading:** A recovering South Asian island economy built on services, tourism, and strategic location, but still marked by crisis-era fragility.
