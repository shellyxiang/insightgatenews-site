# Nepal — Quick Facts


- **Regional logic:**  **mountainous remittance-and-hydropower economy** in South Asia.
- **Basic economic profile:** A **small landlocked economy** with remittances, agriculture, services, and hydropower all playing major roles.
- **Main economic base:** Remittances remain one of the strongest supports for domestic demand and external stability, while hydropower and tourism are increasingly important.
- **Key structural feature:** Nepal is one of the clearest examples of a country whose economy is shaped by **migration income, geography, and infrastructure constraints**.
- **Main current issues:** Growth has improved, but the economy still depends heavily on remittances, imports, and progress in hydropower and infrastructure.
- **Why it matters in the map:** It helps show that South Asia includes **mountain landlocked economies** very different from India or Bangladesh.
- **Best short reading:** A remittance-supported Himalayan economy trying to broaden growth through hydropower, tourism, and construction.
